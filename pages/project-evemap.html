EVE MAP

One fine day Charul (friend of mine) and I were relaxing at our university so we came up with an idea and did some research but it didn't work out so we thought of another idea. This idea would be a fusion of tinder-like features and an event-driven atmosphere. Basically, it was about networking people with similar regional interests at events and thus finding like-minded people for an event to attend to. Unfortunately, this idea did not come true and it still in the construction phase. During the development phase, we thought of an application for the idea (also a website).

I had studied UI/UX so I started working on developing wireframes for the application. After developing these wireframes, I worked on screen mockups for the app.

Concept

When you don't have somebody to accompany you to an event and want someone who shares your enthusiasm for the event. So what exactly do you do? You don't go or go alone, obviously. EVE MAP is here to help with that. Connecting you with individuals who share your interests in events so that you don't miss out on your favorite events or go alone.

The idea for the EVE MAP app came from conducting extensive research into the target demographic and repeating the wireframes to gain a better grasp of how to create a workflow for the app. To provide a rich experience for the audience (i.e. mainly college students between 20-30 years of age). I tried to keep it as minimal and easy as possible.
